Professional Opportunities

What does ASUCD do?

 

ASUCD offers opportunities for students to grow professionally through units, committees, commissions and more.

There are multiple bodies under ASUCD, all of which provide a platform for students to advocate for issues they want to raise awareness to, express themselves creatively, and foster leadership skills.
